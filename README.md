# 🚚 Logistics Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of logistics operations through the development of an interactive Power BI dashboard. Leveraging data from loads, trips, customers, routes, delivery events, and operational costs, the analysis provides insights into revenue performance, profitability, customer contribution, delivery efficiency, and fuel expenses.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze revenue and profitability trends.
- Identify the most profitable routes.
- Evaluate fuel costs across states and months.
- Monitor delivery performance and delayed delivery rates.
- Understand customer contribution to revenue.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve operational efficiency.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains information related to:

- Loads and Trips
- Customers
- Drivers
- Routes
- Revenue and Profit
- Fuel Costs
- Delivery Events
- Load Dates
- Shipment Statuses

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Revenue Growth %
- Total Customers
- Delayed Delivery Rate
- Fuel Cost
- Most Profitable Route

---

## 🔍 Insight Questions Explored

1. How has revenue changed over time?
2. Which routes are the most profitable?
3. Which drivers contribute most to revenue?
4. What is the delayed delivery rate?
5. How do fuel costs vary by state and month?
6. Which customers contribute the most revenue?
7. How has profit changed over time?
8. What factors influence operational performance?

## 💡 Key Insights

- Revenue exhibited a positive growth trend over the analysis period.
- Certain routes contributed significantly to overall profitability.
- Fuel costs varied across states and months, impacting operational expenses.
- A small number of customers accounted for a substantial share of total revenue.
- Delayed deliveries highlighted opportunities for improving operational efficiency.

---

## 📌 Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing routes to maximize profitability.
- Monitor and optimize fuel expenses across regions.
- Improve delivery efficiency to reduce delays.
- Strengthen relationships with high-value customers.
- Continuously monitor KPIs to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 📝 Conclusion

The Logistics Performance Dashboard successfully transformed raw logistics data into meaningful insights. The analysis provided visibility into revenue trends, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency, enabling data-driven decisions and supporting business growth.
